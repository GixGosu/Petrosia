Petrosia

AI-Powered Knowledge Management

Welcome to the MVP Demo

This page demonstrates the embeddable Petrosia chat widget. Look for the chat bubble in the bottom right corner!

Try asking:
  • "How do I reset my password?"
  • "What payment methods do you accept?"
  • "How do I get a refund?"
  • "How do I get started?"

Key Features

  • Single script tag embed - just copy and paste
  • Streaming AI responses powered by RAG
  • Multi-language support (EN, JA, DE, and more)
  • Source citations from knowledge base
  • Graceful "I don't know" when info not available
  • No hallucinations - grounded in actual articles
  • Multi-provider LLM support (Claude, GPT, Gemini, Local)

How to Embed

Add this single line to any webpage:

<script src="http://localhost:8000/static/widget/widget.js" data-api-url="http://localhost:8000"></script>

That's it! No complex setup required.

👉 Click the chat bubble in the bottom right to start!
